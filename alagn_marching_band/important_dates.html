Band Camp

Band camp for 2023 will be a very important one. We will be introduced to our new band director Mr. Freddy Ochoa. Mr. Ochoa will be eager to get this season started, and we are looking forward to seeing what exciting performances Mr. Ochoa has planned. Band camp for the 2023 season will have a two day pre-camp, and a five day full day camp.

Thursday July 13, Marching Band Pre-Camp. New members from 4p-8p. All members from 6p-8p. Meet in the auditorium.

Friday July 14, Marching Band Pre-Camp and Parent Meeting. New Members from 4p-8p. All Members from 6p-8p. Parent meeting from 7:30p-8p. Meet in the auditorium.

Monday July 17, Marching Band Camp. 9a-9p. Meet in the auditorium. Lunch and dinner will be provided.

Tuesday July 18, Marching Band Camp. 9a-9p. Meet in the auditorium. Lunch and dinner will be provided.

Wednesday July 19, Marching Band Camp. 9a-9p. Meet in the auditorium. Lunch and dinner will be provided.

Thursday July 20, Marching Band Camp. 9a-9p. Meet in the auditorium. Lunch and dinner will be provided.

Friday July 21, Marching Band Camp, Preview Show and Family BBQ. 9a-9p. Meet in the auditorium. Lunch will be provided. Family BBQ will be after the preview show at 7:30p

Uniform for band camp will be athletic shoes, white socks, shorts, and short sleeve shirt(no tank tops). Hats and sunglasses are encouraged. Please bring a water bottle. Ice, water and cooling towels will be provided

Football Games

The 2023 marching season will be a fun one when we bring our talents and music to Eagles football games. The Eagles varsity football team will be defending their 2022 state title. This season we will move up to battle in the 5A conference. This will bring a whole new challenge with new teams and new rivalries. The season for the Marching Eagles is broken up into two seasons for our uniform. The season starts on August 25. We will wear our summer uniform through the september 22nd game. Starting on the October 6th game, we will wear our marching uniforms through the end of the season.

Summer uniform: black shoes, black socks, black shorts and our 2023 marching band shirt.

Marching uniforms: please wear your summer uniform under your marching uniform.
