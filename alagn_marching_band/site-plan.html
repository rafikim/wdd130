Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our site is to provide the necessary information to have a successful marching band season. The information provided will be everything that is needed for the entire season.

Audience

The intended audience for this site will be family members of marching band students, students, boosters, volunteers, directors, faculty and anyone else who is interested in our marching band. Interested sponsors will be able to see where the band will be performing, and what kind of audience will see their product.

Content

Home page

We are the Marching Eagles of American Leadership Academy Gilbert North. On this page there will be information about the marching band. Who are the Marching Eagles? Why are they the Marching Eagles? What do the Marching Eagles do? This page will answer those questions, and highlight accomplishments of the Marching Eagles. There will also be information on who to contact for additional information. Visitors here should expect to see pictures of what we have done, and what it is that we plan to do. On this page, visitors will be able to see some of our product, along with information of our directors. Visitors to this page will be students, guardians, faculty, administrators, sponsors, and other interested people. Visitors will come to this page for information about the band.

Images for the Home page

Calendar

The calendar will be the most important page for our visitors. Here our visitors will be able to find all the important information of what our students will be doing when, and where. This page will have important information about marching band camp dates, marching competition dates, football game dates, marching band fee payment dates, and any other important dates as needed. Also with this information will be uniform information. This page will help all interested parties to know the important information about upcoming events so that they can plan to attend or share with family and friends. This calendar will also be useful when planning important family events or appointments. The calendar will also be very useful for sponsors, or people who are interested in sponsoring our marching band. The information will be very useful to see where we will be on certain dates, and who will see our marching band. This page should be the most visited page on the website due to the importance of the information that is available on here. Visitors that will be visiting this page will be students, guardians, faculty, administrators, sponsors, volunteers, boosters, and other interested people looking for information about where to see our marching band.

Images for the Page 2

How To Help The Band

This page will be a more select page for visitors. This page will have important information for volunteers, booster, sponsors, interested volunteers, interested sponsors, and other people interested in helping our marching band to be successful. Important information for volunteers, or people interested in becoming a volunteer will be available here. There will be information on positions available, and what each position does. There will be ways to find out how to volunteer, and why volunteering is very important to the success of the marching band. There will be information about how there are other ways to volunteer if giving of your time is not something that can be done. There will be information on boosters. This information will be about what boosters do, position descriptions, and how to become a booster if interested. Why are boosters necessary, and how do they help the marching band. There will be information on who the boosters are, and how to contact them for information. There will be a section about sponsors. This information will have names of sponsors, and important information about their product if they are a company. There will also be very important information for companies, families, or individuals who are interested in sponsoring the marching band. There will be information about how to sponsor. In the information, it will go over what you get with your sponsorhip, and how your brand or information will be seen. The information will also talk about what the marching band will be able to recieve or do with the sponsorship funds. The marching band would not be able to operate without the important help from the boosters time and effort, volunteers time and effot, and sponsorhip funds that are generously donated to help the marching band to be a successful program.
